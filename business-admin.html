Overview

The Business Administration Department at BrightFuture College provides a comprehensive education in management principles and practices. Our programs are designed to develop future leaders and entrepreneurs.

Faculty

Our faculty members are highly qualified professionals with extensive industry experience and academic expertise. Dr. Rina Kapoor, with a PhD in Management, leads the department.

Facilities

The department features smart classrooms, a modern library, case-study labs, and access to digital business simulation tools that enhance the learning experience.

Courses covered in each semester

  • Semester 1: Principles of Management, Business Economics, Accounting
  • Semester 2: Organizational Behavior, Statistics, Marketing Basics
  • Semester 3: HR Management, Business Law, Finance
  • Semester 4: Strategic Management, Operations, Entrepreneurship
  • Semester 5: International Business, Research Methods, MIS
  • Semester 6: Leadership, Business Ethics, Financial Services
  • Semester 7: Project Work, Internship, Electives
  • Semester 8: Comprehensive Viva, Seminar, Case Studies

Research

The department encourages research in areas like corporate governance, behavioral economics, sustainable business practices, and strategic leadership. Students actively participate in national and international conferences.

Career Opportunities

Graduates pursue careers in consulting, finance, HR, marketing, and entrepreneurship. Leading companies such as Deloitte, EY, KPMG, and HDFC frequently recruit from our campus.
