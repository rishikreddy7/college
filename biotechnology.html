Overview

The Biotechnology Department at BrightFuture College provides interdisciplinary education combining biology, chemistry, and technology to create innovative solutions in medicine, agriculture, and environmental sustainability.

Facilities

Our department is equipped with advanced biotechnology labs for genetic engineering, microbiology, biochemistry, and bioinformatics with real-time research capabilities.

Courses covered in each semester

  • Semester 1: Cell Biology, Biochemistry, Environmental Science
  • Semester 2: Microbiology, Molecular Biology, Analytical Techniques
  • Semester 3: Immunology, Genetic Engineering, Biostatistics
  • Semester 4: Bioinformatics, Enzyme Technology, Plant Biotechnology
  • Semester 5: Animal Biotechnology, Bioprocess Engineering, Research Methodology
  • Semester 6: Genomics, Industrial Biotechnology, Electives
  • Semester 7: Internship, Seminar, Project Phase 1
  • Semester 8: Project Phase 2, Viva, Elective Courses

Research

Students are encouraged to work on research in genetic engineering, cancer biology, fermentation technology, and environmental biotechnology under expert guidance.

Career Opportunities

Graduates can pursue careers in pharmaceutical industries, agricultural biotech firms, research institutions, or opt for higher studies and R&D positions globally.
