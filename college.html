Admissions are based on entrance exams followed by a counseling session. Visit our admissions page for more details.

Companies like Infosys, TCS, Deloitte, L&T, and Adobe regularly recruit from our campus.

Yes, we offer merit-based and need-based scholarships. Details can be found on our scholarships page.
